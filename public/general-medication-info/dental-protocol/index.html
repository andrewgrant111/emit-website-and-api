Dental Protocol

The following information can be provided to your dentist:

Prophylactic antibiotic coverage for dental procedures is recommended ONLY for the following transplant recipients:
  • • Prosthetic cardiac valve
  • • Previous infective endocarditis
  • • Congenital heart disease (CHD)
  • • Unrepaired cyanotic CHD
  • • 6 months following repair of CHD with any prosthetic material or device
  • • Repaired CHD with residual defects
  • • Cardiac transplant recipients who develop cardiac valvulopathy
